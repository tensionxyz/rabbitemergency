About RabbitEmergency.com

RabbitEmergency.com helps worried rabbit owners across Asia recognise urgent warning signs, reach a rabbit-savvy or exotic veterinarian quickly, and understand the recovery support a vet may recommend afterwards.

We are an educational resource, not a veterinary practice. We do not diagnose or treat rabbits and we never position any product as a substitute for veterinary care. Our goal is to help you decide when to seek urgent care and what information to prepare before you call a clinic.

What we cover

Plain-language triage for the emergencies that matter most in rabbits — gut stasis, bloat, heatstroke, flystrike, head tilt, dental disease, and post-operative care — plus guidance on vet-led recovery support from the WOOLY daily-care range and the Alfavet RodiCare veterinary series.

RabbitEmergency.com is published by CCH Foundation, a Hong Kong registered charity.

RabbitEmergency.com is an educational resource and does not provide veterinary diagnosis or treatment. In an emergency, contact a rabbit-savvy veterinarian immediately.
