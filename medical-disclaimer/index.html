Medical disclaimer

RabbitEmergency.com provides general educational information about rabbit emergencies. It is not veterinary advice and is not a substitute for examination, diagnosis, or treatment by a qualified veterinarian.

If you think your rabbit has an emergency, contact a rabbit-savvy or exotic veterinarian immediately. Never delay seeking care because of something you read here.

Products mentioned on this site are supportive options that a veterinarian may recommend after assessing your rabbit. They are not medicines for self-treating an emergency, and nothing on this site should be read as a recommendation to treat your rabbit at home in place of veterinary care.

We make no warranty that the information is complete or current for your rabbit's individual situation. Always follow your own veterinarian's instructions.
