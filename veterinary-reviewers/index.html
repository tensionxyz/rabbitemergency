What reviewers check

  • Emergency thresholds and owner-safe wording.
  • Claims about recovery products stay supportive, never treatment claims.
  • Sources are consistent with RWAF, House Rabbit Society, Merck Vet Manual, Cornell, VCA, and exotic small-mammal practice standards.
  • Clinic pages include source links, last verification dates, and call-before-travel warnings.
