What to say when you call

  • I have a rabbit emergency. Do you treat rabbits or exotic small mammals right now?
  • State the main sign and how long it has been happening.
  • Ask whether to come immediately, what to bring, and whether another clinic is better if no rabbit vet is on duty.
  • Confirm the address, phone number, after-hours fee, and intake process.

Before travelling to a Singapore clinic

  • Call first: Do not assume 24h means exotic-capable
  • Confirm: emergency hours, rabbit/exotic capability, current vet availability, address, and whether oxygen or urgent stabilization is available.
  • Tell the clinic: City, rabbit sign, current urgency, clinic hours, species capability, phone result, and last verified date
  • Verification standard: each listing should keep a source link, visible caveat, last-verified date, and correction route.
