How our guidance is reviewed

Rabbit medicine is a specialist field. Our content is source-cited and pending named veterinary review so that owners get guidance consistent with current rabbit-welfare and exotic small-mammal medicine standards.

Our review standards

  • RWAF (Rabbit Welfare Association & Fund)
  • House Rabbit Society
  • Merck Veterinary Manual and peer exotic small-mammal references

Review process

Each guide is checked for clinical accuracy, safe triage advice, and claim discipline before publication and carries a last-reviewed date. Congress partner clinics may also publish doctor-reviewed guidance under their own profile.

Review confirms that guidance is consistent with recognised standards. It does not create a veterinarian–client–patient relationship or replace your own vet's assessment of your rabbit.
