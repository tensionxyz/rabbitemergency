Correction policy

If a clinic detail, emergency threshold, source citation, translation, or product-support statement is inaccurate, RabbitEmergency.com handles the correction as urgent editorial work.

What to send

  • The page URL and exact phrase or clinic listing.
  • The correction requested and a public source, clinic page, or reviewer note.
  • Whether the issue is safety-critical, such as hours, address, phone, or emergency capability.

Correction priority

  • Same day: unsafe medical wording, clinic emergency capability, phone/address/hours, or translation that changes triage meaning.
  • Within 7 days: source updates, reviewer bio changes, wording clarity, formatting issues.
  • Monthly: routine clinic verification and sitemap freshness.

Visible update trail

Medical and clinic pages show last reviewed, last updated, or last verified dates. Substantial changes are reflected in sitemap freshness and llms.txt discovery.
